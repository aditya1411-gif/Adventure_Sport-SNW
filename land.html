Hiking

Hiking is a recreational activity that involves walking long distances on well-marked trails, usually through natural landscapes like forests, hills, or the countryside.

Mountaineering

Mountaineering is the sport or activity of climbing mountains using technical skills and equipment such as ropes, ice axes, and crampons. It involves a combination of hiking, rock climbing, and ice climbing, often at high altitudes where environmental conditions and objective hazards like avalanches, falling ice, and altitude sickness pose significant risks.

Skiing

Skiing is the competitive winter sport and recreational activity of gliding over snow on skis. It is split into two main types: alpine (downhill) and Nordic (cross-country) skiing, though competitive events also include freestyle and snowboarding.

Dirt Biking

Dirt biking is an adventurous outdoor sport that involves riding specialized, lightweight motorcycles on rugged, unpaved terrain such as dirt trails, hills, mud, and sand. It is a physically demanding activity enjoyed for both recreation and competitive racing.
